Welcome to the Assessment Management System

Manage and track your assessments efficiently

This system helps employees register for assessments and enables the Assessment Team Administrator to manage registrations. You can choose between two types of assessments:

  • Technical Assessments (e.g., Java, C#, PHP)
  • Behavioral Assessments (e.g., Step Plus, Learning Agility)

Employees can register for assessments and view their progress, while administrators can track all employee registrations.
